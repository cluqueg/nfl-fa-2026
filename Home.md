# Welcome to the NFL Free Agency 2026 Project Wiki

## Overview
This project aims to provide comprehensive insights and tools related to the NFL free agency period of 2026, focusing on player movement, contract analysis, and team strategies.

### Purpose
The primary goal of this project is to offer a centralized platform where fans, analysts, and teams can track player signings, negotiations, and other vital information during the free agency period.

### How to Use
- **Exploring Player Transactions:** Navigate through the pages to find detailed information on player signings and trades.
- **Analyzing Contracts:** Access tools and resources designed to analyze player contracts and predict team strategies.
- **Contributing:** If you'd like to contribute, please refer to the contribution guidelines below.
